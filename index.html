Oxford High School

Being a Wildcat

Here at Oxford High School (OHS), we are all Wildcats. We work hard, try our best, help each other, and get involved.

One thing that I personally like about our school is our courtyards. They are spacious and are typically open, except for during the winter for the most part, allowing us to cut through them on our way to class. And in recent years due to social distancing, we have even been allowed to eat lunch out there with our friends.

Our school also provides a wide vareity of classes. Some of my favorites include:

  1. Web Design
  2. Advanced Studio
  3. Digital Imaging Technologies
  4. Financial Management
If you can't find the class you are looking for, you can find through our district's online school, Oxford Virtual Academy and will be able to take that class during school hours in one of our many computer labs.
